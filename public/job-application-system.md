# Job Application System — Daily Pipeline

## Your Interview Hook

> "I built a SmartBank backend system using Java Spring Boot that simulates banking infrastructure with microservices, secure transactions, and event-driven design. It focuses on system design, consistency, and scalability principles."

---

## Daily Workflow (30 min)

1. Open LinkedIn Jobs — filter "Java" + "Backend" + "Entry Level" (5 min)
2. Open company career pages below — check new grad/junior roles (5 min)
3. Pick 5-10 roles from your tracker's "New" column (5 min)
4. Apply using template below — 2 min each (10-20 min)
5. Log results in tracker (2 min)

---

## Follow-up Rule

- No response after 7 days → send a polite follow-up email
- No response after 14 days → move to "Ghosted" — keep applying
- Rejection → ask for feedback politely; do not argue

---

## Your Pipeline Metrics

- 10 applications submitted = 1 screening call (average)
- 5 screening calls = 1 technical interview
- 3 technical interviews = 1 final round
- 2 final rounds = 1 offer

Apply to 50+ to get your first offer. No exceptions.
